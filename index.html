  • Minesweeper

    Made using:

    During the 2020 pandemic, I developed a Minesweeper clone, one of the first games I created in Unity.

    The premise is simple: click on all the empty squares on the board without hitting any mines. The numbers on the squares indicate the number of neighboring mines.
